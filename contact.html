Q: How do I report a broken game?

A: Use the contact form above with the "Bug Report" subject and tell us which game isn't working and what device you're using.

Q: Can you add my favorite game?

A: Absolutely! Send us a game suggestion with the name and a brief description. We're always looking to expand our collection.
