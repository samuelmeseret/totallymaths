🚀 Run 3 - 3D Spatial Navigation Adventure

Master 3D geometry and spatial reasoning as you navigate through space tunnels! This educational adventure teaches coordinate systems, geometric transformations, and strategic planning while challenging your spatial awareness skills.

✅ Curriculum Aligned 📚 Grades 4-10 🎯 3D Geometry 🧭 Spatial Reasoning ⏱️ 20-40 minutes
⭐ 4.9/5 Educational Value 🎮 Adventure Learning 🧠 Spatial Skills 🏫 Teacher Approved

📚 Learning Objectives & Educational Value

🎯 Primary Learning Goals

  • 3D Spatial Reasoning: Understand how objects move in three-dimensional space
  • Coordinate Systems: Learn about X, Y, and Z axes in 3D space
  • Geometric Transformations: Experience rotations and translations firsthand
  • Strategic Planning: Develop forward-thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Gravity Concepts: Understand how gravity affects movement in different orientations

📐 Mathematical Concepts

  • Understanding perspective and viewpoint changes
  • Spatial orientation and directional awareness
  • Pattern recognition in tunnel layouts
  • Distance estimation and path optimization
  • Cause and effect relationships in physics

🧠 Cognitive Skills Developed

  • Mental rotation abilities
  • Visual-spatial processing
  • Problem-solving under pressure
  • Planning and strategy development
  • Hand-eye coordination

🎮 How to Play & Learn

🕹️ Controls

Movement: Arrow keys or WASD to move

Jump: Spacebar to jump

Special: Run on walls and ceilings!

🎯 Educational Goals

Observe: How gravity changes based on your position

Plan: Think ahead about your path through the tunnel

Learn: Understanding 3D space and orientation

💡 Learning Tips

Strategy: Plan your route carefully using spatial reasoning

Physics: Notice how gravity affects your movement

Geometry: Use tunnel walls strategically for navigation

👩‍🏫 Discussion Questions & Activities

🗣️ Pre-Game Discussion

  • What do you know about 3D space and coordinate systems?
  • How does gravity work in our everyday environment?
  • What strategies might help you navigate a 3D tunnel?
  • How do you think changing perspective affects spatial awareness?

🤔 Post-Game Reflection

  • How did your understanding of 3D space change while playing?
  • What strategies worked best for navigating the tunnels?
  • How did gravity changes affect your movement planning?
  • Can you describe the coordinate system used in the game?

📝 Extension Activities

  • Draw a map of a tunnel level from different perspectives
  • Create your own 3D maze using coordinate geometry
  • Research how astronauts navigate in zero gravity
  • Design a strategy guide using geometric principles
