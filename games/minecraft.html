🎮 How to Play Minecraft

Movement: WASD keys to move, Mouse to look around, Space to jump

Actions: Left click to break blocks, Right click to place blocks/interact

Inventory: E key to open inventory, 1-9 keys to select hotbar items

Crafting: Combine materials in your inventory to create new items and tools

Survival Tips: Gather wood first, make tools, find shelter before nightfall!

Creative Mode: Unlimited resources and flying ability for building
